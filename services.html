Proper storage is key to maintaining the freshness, flavor, and integrity of your Bake Delights cake. For most of our cakes featuring classic buttercreams or tangy cream cheese frostings, refrigeration is recommended to preserve their quality and structural stability. To ensure the best possible flavor and moist texture, we advise bringing these cakes to room temperature for approximately 30 to 60 minutes before serving. Cakes adorned with fondant, however, should typically be stored at room temperature in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight or excessive heat, as refrigeration can sometimes cause condensation. Always store your cake in an airtight container or its original box to prevent it from drying out or absorbing other odors from your refrigerator.
